Grow your skills to advance your career path

The purpose of the Texas Tech Google Developer Club is to make technology more accessible and we do that by hosting workshops, code-alongs, and speaker events! We also organize open source projects for members to participate in. The goal is to bridge the gap between theory and application by gaining real-life experience, building projects to help the community, and making students ready for taking the first step into their professional career.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GDSC Texas Tech University?

GDSC stands for Google Developer Student Clubs. It is a program designed to help students learn and improve their technical skills, as well as providing them with networking opportunities with professionals in the tech industry.

Who can join GDSC Texas Tech University?

Any current student of Texas Tech University can join GDSC Texas Tech University.

What are the benefits of joining GDSC Texas Tech University?

Joining GDSC Texas Tech University provides students with access to various resources such as workshops, mentorship, and networking opportunities. Additionally, being a member of GDSC Texas Tech University can enhance your resume and improve your technical skills.

Do I need to have prior technical knowledge to join GDSC TTU?

No, prior technical knowledge is not required to join GDSC Texas Tech University. GDSC Texas Tech University welcomes members with all levels of technical knowledge.

How can I join GDSC Texas Tech University?

You can join GDSC Texas Tech University by filling out the membership application form found on the club's official website or by attending one of the club's events.

Is there a membership fee to join GDSC Texas Tech University?

No, there is no membership fee to join GDSC Texas Tech University.

How can I stay up-to-date with GDSC TTU events and activities?

You can stay up-to-date with GDSC Texas Tech University events and activities by following the club's social media pages, Discord, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn or signing up for our newsletter on TechConnect. Additionally, you can attend one of their events and express your interest in being added to our mailing list.

What kind of events and workshops does GDSC Texas Tech University offer?

GDSC Texas Tech University offers various events and workshops focused on technical topics such as coding, machine learning, and web development. Additionally, GDSC Texas Tech University hosts events focused on soft skills such as public speaking and networking.

How can I become a mentor for GDSC Texas Tech University?

To become a mentor for GDSC Texas Tech University, you can contact the club's leadership team through their official website or attend one of their events and express your interest in becoming a mentor.

Can non-Texas Tech University students attend GDSC TTU events?

Non-Texas Tech University students are welcome to attend GDSC Texas Tech University events.
